Luogu Auto Submit

Bridge your terminal to Luogu — submit code from the command line, auto-evaluate in the browser.

Step 1: Start the daemon

In your terminal: pnpm --filter @luogu-auto/daemon dev

Step 2: Connection Status

Checking...

Extension connects to ws://127.0.0.1:9528

Step 3: Usage

From your terminal:
luogu-cli submit P1001.cpp
luogu-cli batch ./source/website/www.luogu.com.cn/

Step 4: Important

You must be logged in to Luogu in your browser. The extension reuses your existing session.
